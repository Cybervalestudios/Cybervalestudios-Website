Among The Walls New Game!!

Posted Nov 12, 2025 • by CyberVale Studios

It’s finally happening — CyberVale Studios has been hard at work behind the scenes on our next major title Among the Walls.

Among The Walls?

Step into the tiny paws of a hyper-intelligent mouse in Among the Walls, a unique survival adventure set within the hidden corners of a bustling apartment complex. After escaping a scientist’s lab where you've been genetically enhanced to be smarter than any human, your mission is clear: survive, explore, and uncover secrets—without ever being caught.

In Among the Walls, you begin as a lab mouse in a scientist’s cage. After a helped escape, you find yourself in a sprawling apartment complex filled with danger, mystery, and opportunity. Your goal? Survive, explore, and gather intel—without ever being caught.

Customize Your Mouse - Choose your mouse type, Pick your fur color, body shape, and more.

Apartment Survival - Explore kitchens, rooftops, and hidden crawlspaces.Each condo has unique residents like cats dos snakes + more. Scavenge trash cans for random loot: buttons, lint, parts, and surprises.

Build Your Base Dig for resources like wood chips and pebbles. Construct mouse holes, furniture, and decor. Wearables like hats and shoes require crafting materials.

Climb and Explore Use suction cups and other tools to scale walls and reach new areas.

Multiplayer Mode?! (coming soon?) Play with friends as a team of mice. Build together, explore together, survive together.
