New Game Reveal Coming Soon!

Posted October 28, 2025 • by CyberVale Studios

It’s finally happening — CyberVale Studios has been hard at work behind the scenes on our next major title. While we can’t share all the details just yet, we’re thrilled to officially confirm that a new game reveal is coming soon.

What We Can Tell You

This upcoming project dives deeper into the themes that define CyberVale — mysterious worlds, powerful atmospheres, and gameplay that blends exploration with emotional storytelling. Expect a bold new world with a visual identity that pushes our engine to its limits.

Reveal Trailer Incoming

Our first teaser trailer is no where near complete and will not premiere in the coming weeks. The reveal will showcase early gameplay and give you a first glimpse at the story and setting. Keep an eye on our social channels for the official announcement date!

Behind the Scenes

The game is currently in pre-alpha, with our small (me) but passionate team experimenting with new tools and design systems. We’re focusing on world interactivity, player choice, and immersion — aiming to create something both personal and awe-inspiring.

Stay Connected

Be sure to follow us on our Socials and join the community on Discord to stay up to date. We can’t wait to share what we’ve been building — and this is just the beginning.
